About

We serve a wholesome, freshly made meal each Saturday – one that we are happy to eat ourselves – in a respectful atmosphere to anyone who comes through our doors, no questions asked. We provide a welcoming place of respite for our guests and offer vital social services to those in need. We do this with an all-volunteer staff, without a single employee.

Volunteer

University Community Social Services is staffed entirely by volunteers. There would be no soup kitchen without you!

Donate

University Community Social Services is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization that depends on donations from people like you to continue our work. Despite our name, we are not affiliated with any university, nor are we affiliated with any religious organization. We are all-volunteer run, without a single employee, so 95 cents of every dollar you give goes towards providing meals and services to our guests.
